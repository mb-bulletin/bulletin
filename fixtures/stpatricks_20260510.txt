ARE YOU MARRIED BY THE STATE BUT NOT YET BY THE CATHOLIC CHURCH?
Reach out to rosa@oldcathedral.org about having your marriage convalidated in the Church! Having God's blessing on your marriage will strengthen and uplift it. This would allow you to receive communion at Mass as well. It doesn't require having a big ceremony. Also, we will never turn anyone away for lack of the ability to pay. Call the parish office during normal office hours to inquire!

BOOK CLUB
You are warmly invited each Sunday of the month to our book club! On May 10, 5:50pm at 32 Prince St, we begin our study of The City of God by Saint Augustine. Written in the 5th century as the Roman Empire was in decline, this work is a foundational defense of the faith that explores the relationship between the divine and the earthly. It is a profound example of how Catholic interior life has influenced the modern view of what it means to be human.

TOUR OUR CATACOMBS
Take a tour of our catacombs. Would you like to learn about our Old Cathedral's rich history? Join one of our Catacombs by Candlelight Tours. In just 90 minutes you will walk through two centuries of history experiencing exclusive treasures that are not open to the public. Visit www.oldcathedral.org and click on the tours link.

BURIAL SERVICES AT THE BASILICA
We have revived the tradition of Christian Burial here at Old St. Patrick's as the only operating Catholic graveyard and catacombs in Manhattan. There are a limited number of niches available. Email Frank Alfieri at frank@oldcathedral.org or call (212) 226-8075 to learn more.
